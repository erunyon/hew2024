@import

Can I Use: 96%

The @import is used to import style rules from other valid stylesheets. An @import rule must be defined at the top of the stylesheet, before any other at-rule (except @charset and @layer) and style declarations, or it will be ignored.

It's been around…


@import url("dead-trees.css") print;

But now it can do this…


@import url("screen.css") screen and (min-width: 800px);

@import url("grid.css") supports(display: grid)
        screen and (width >= 400px);

@import url("flex.css") supports((not (display: grid))
        and (display: flex)) screen and (width >= 400px);

@import url(cards.css) layer(components);
@import url(headings.css) layer(headings);
